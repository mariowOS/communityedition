Data & privacy
Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

We at mariowOS respect your privacy deeply. No personal data is sold, shared, or transmitted to third parties without your consent (when using online features)

During setup, mariowOS may store basic configuration preferences locally on your device (such as appearance mode and language) to improve your experience.

Network connections are only used when explicitly required for updates or for online features. Telemetry, analytics, or tracking services are not enabled at ALL.

Local storage

Some settings (including theme preference) are stored locally in your browser or environment using local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-party resources

mariowOS may include open-source components and assets from trusted providers. Each component follows its own license and privacy terms.

For more information visit: https://doc.gg/mariowOS
